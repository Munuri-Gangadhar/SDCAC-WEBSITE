ABOUT US

Student Development & Campus Activities cell (SDCAC) of Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies-Nuzvid promotes the integral part that occurs outside the classroom through co-curricular activities. It mainly focuses on overall individual development of students like leadership qualities, team work, communication skills, creative thinking, entrepreneurship qualities, ethics, moral values and professionalism.

About us

Student Development and Campus Activity Cell solely called as SDCAC by the students of RGUKT Nuzvid, is the prestigious organization of the University working for overall individual development of students through co-curricular activities.

Furthermore, SDCAC is the organization of RGUKT which aims to enlighten the students by taking the responsibility of directing National Level Technical Fest - TECKZITE every year.

SDCAC, Student Development and Campus Activity Cell- is not only a platform to prove one's own talents but also the precious opportunity to show one's true potential to unleash themselves. This is the organization which received a greater acclamation and outstanding feedback for its upshot in most of the activities. For many of the IIIT'ns, SDCAC is an aspiring organization in which everyone dreams to take part by becoming a member or by giving a participation. It's like a journey where one can find the abilities and talents of oneself which are usually hidden under their fears and insecurities and promises to change one's perspective towards technology and engineering.

It goes with the objective of "bridging the gap between the senior and junior students" in the campus. We offer many opportunities for student involvement through student organizations, campus events, conferences, and much more. This initiation provides experiences for students to identify their goals, discover passions, groups to act responsibly and accept accountability for their words actions and behaviors

History

As there is a lack of interaction between final year students and third year students, most of the problems and hurdles which are faced by final years are getting repeated by juniors also. Regarding branch allocation there are many misconceptions. To create awareness to the students just only by faculty is becoming a heavy task. So, by initiating student development cell in the campus Student Development Cell was started on 28th November,2013 by students under the sincere guidance of faculty.

The following are the major issues to be discussed at SDC.

  • GATE/CAT/GRE/TOEFEL
  • Projects
  • Internships
  • Subjects/Labs
  • Tech-fests
  • GD/Personal Interviews
  • Branch Allocation
  • Any other subject related issues

Later in the coming years considering the hustle and need of the students, to promote and adopt to the coming technologies SDC was amended as SDCAC [Student Development and Campus Activity Cell]
